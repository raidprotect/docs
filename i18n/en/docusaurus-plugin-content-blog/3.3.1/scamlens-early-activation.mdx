### What's next? {#next}

The `Sanction` mode will be activated soon: in addition to deleting fraudulent images, ScamLens will automatically apply a **one-day timeout** to the sender. We chose not to ban these accounts, as they are almost exclusively **hacked accounts** â€” a ban would penalize the actual account owner. The timeout neutralizes the threat immediately, giving the owner time to regain control of their account.
