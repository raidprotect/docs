It primarily targets **cryptocurrency**, **crypto casino** and **fake investment** scams, spread massively through hacked Discord accounts.

## 🆕 Updates {#updates}

### 🪵 Detection logs {#logs}

ScamLens now has a **dedicated log**: each detection of a fraudulent image generates a message in your logs channel, with the member involved and the targeted channel(s). You know exactly what the bot intercepted, with no additional configuration.

### 🔍 Extended coverage {#coverage}

Some images were previously slipping through analysis due to the way they were served by Discord's CDN. ScamLens now covers these cases as well.

## ❓ FAQ {#faq}

#### Why do image scams appear on my Discord server? {#pourquoi-scam-images}
These images are sent by **hacked Discord accounts**. Scammers compromise accounts, then use them to spam as many servers as possible at once. Your server is **not specifically targeted** — it's part of the batch. A single hacked account can reach **dozens of servers in seconds**, which is why automatic removal is essential.

#### How do I protect my Discord server against scams? {#proteger-serveur-scam-discord}
[Add RaidProtect](https://raidprotect.bot/invite) to your server. ScamLens is active by default and automatically removes images detected as fraudulent, with no configuration required.

#### Do scam images evolve to bypass detection? {#evolution-scam}
Yes, techniques change regularly. ScamLens is continuously updated to keep up with these evolutions.
